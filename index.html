Clownfish
Clownfish are vibrant,
orange-and-white striped marine fish famous
for their symbiotic relationship
with sea anemones.
star fish
Starfish, scientifically known as sea stars,
are star-shaped marine invertebrates found on the seafloor
of every ocean on Earth.
they are not true fish.

green sea turtle
The green sea turtle
is the largest species of hard-shelled sea turtle
and a vital keystone species for marine ecosystems.

Coral reefs
Coral reefs are massive
underwater structures built by colonies
of tiny animals called coral polyps.
